◎ The mission

Build your own hydrophone from accessible parts and use it to detect, then localize and track, the resident sperm whales off western Mauritius — legally and ethically. Because hardware is slow to reach the island, the course runs theory-first: understand everything, order parts up front, then build once they arrive. Glossary · Shopping List · Resources
