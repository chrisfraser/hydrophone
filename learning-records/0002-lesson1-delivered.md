# Lesson 1 delivered — the hook (not yet demonstrated)

Built Lesson 1 "The Loudest Animal in the Sea": a screen-only hook (no build needed) covering the Mauritius
resident population, the sperm-whale click, click types by ICI, spherical spreading (−6 dB/doubling), and the
ethics/law of listening from outside the 100 m zone. Two interactives: a WebAudio click-train player +
waveform/spectrogram canvas, and a detection-range calculator (spherical spreading + ~1 dB/km absorption).

**Editorial decision:** the research synth proposed a hands-on $30 build as lesson 1, but a build isn't
*completable quickly* (parts + drying time), so the build was bumped to Lesson 2 and a same-day conceptual hook
leads instead. Curriculum renumbered around this on the [[index.html]] map (16 lessons, two tracks).
